# Fallback Wallpaper

Place a fallback wallpaper image here named `fallback.png` or `fallback.jpg`.

This will be used as the immediate background while AI wallpaper generates.

Recommended specs:
- Size: 1920x1080 or larger
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Style: Retro pixel art or solid color with subtle texture
- Work-safe, professional

If no fallback exists, a gradient will be used.
